Our Equipment

We pride ourselves on being a friendly, inclusive club offering hockey for all. We welcome everyone from aspiring internationals to social players or those who are new to the game.

Hockey Training

Woking Hockey Club is one of the largest hockey clubs in the country with ten men’s teams, six ladies teams, a mixed side, a range of youth teams, various touring & summer league sides and Back to Hockey sessions.

Private Lessons

Our internationally experienced coaches enable everyone at the club to fulfil their potential at every age group, from juniors starting the game to veterans who are grateful to finish a match.

Ski Touring

​Woking Hockey clubs has superb facilities; two flood-lit, sand-dressed international standard pitches, and our on-site clubhouse provides changing and shower facilities, a cafe and a bar.
